Utah Jazz Legends: Then and Now

John Stockton

John Stockton, known for his incredible passing and defensive skills, is widely regarded as one of the greatest point guards in NBA history. He spent his entire 19-season career with the Utah Jazz, leading the league in assists for nine consecutive seasons.

Karl Malone

Karl Malone, nicknamed "The Mailman," was a dominant power forward who played 18 seasons in the NBA, primarily with the Utah Jazz. Known for his scoring ability and physical play, Malone is second on the NBA's all-time scoring list.

Donovan Mitchell

Donovan Mitchell is a dynamic shooting guard known for his scoring prowess and athleticism. Since being drafted by the Utah Jazz in 2017, he has become one of the team's key players and a fan favorite.

Rudy Gobert

Rudy Gobert, a dominant center known for his shot-blocking and rebounding abilities, has been a crucial part of the Utah Jazz's defense. He has won multiple Defensive Player of the Year awards and continues to be a significant presence in the paint.

Current Utah Jazz Stars

Lauri Markkanen

Lauri Markkanen is a versatile power forward known for his shooting ability and agility. Since joining the Utah Jazz, he has become an essential part of the team's offense and a reliable scorer.

Keyonte George

Keyonte George is an emerging talent known for his scoring and playmaking skills. As a recent addition to the Utah Jazz, he is expected to make a significant impact in the coming seasons.

Ace Bailey

Ace Bailey is a promising young player known for his athleticism and defensive capabilities. As part of the Utah Jazz roster, he is expected to develop into a key contributor for the team.
