Lauri Markkanen

Lauri Markkanen is a versatile Finnish forward known for his shooting ability and scoring versatility. He was drafted from Arizona in the 2017 draft with the 7th overall pick. He was acquired in the Mitchell and Gobert trade in the 2022 offseason and has flourished as a key player for the Jazz. He was an NBA All-Star in 2023 and continues to be a significant contributor to the team's success.
